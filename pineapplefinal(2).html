Before we dive into this topic, let's take a look into the history behind the infamous pineapple pizza.

Pineapple pizza was invented by Sam Panopoulos in 1962. Though pineapple pizza is often associated with Hawaii, it was actually invented in a small resturaunt in Ontario, Canada.

Since then, pineapple pizza took the world by storm, inspiring a new generation of pizza toppings. Although this creation was very popular, it also sparked a lot of drama from a group of particularly angry individuals (you know who you are)...
